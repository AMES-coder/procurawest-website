The Real Cost of Unmanaged Procurement

What is your procurement spend actually costing you?

Most companies leave 5–10% on the table every year and never know it.

Why Procura West

“I'm not Deloitte showing up with a team of juniors and a $150K invoice. The person you talk to is the person who does the work.”

  • 13+ years of hands-on procurement experience in oil & gas and construction
  • Built for companies too big to wing it, too small for a procurement department
  • Calgary-based with deep Western Canadian market expertise
  • No platform to sell, no software subscription — just operational results you can measure

Where We Find Savings

  • Vendor renegotiation — most companies never get competitive bids on existing vendors (8–12% typical)
  • Scope creep recovery — unbilled change orders leak 5–10% per project in construction
  • Duplicate & maverick spending — multiple people buying the same things at different prices from different vendors
  • Payment term optimization — early payment discounts nobody’s capturing
  • Contract compliance — working under expired or poorly structured agreements
