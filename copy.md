## 2. The Procurement Problem

**Section heading:** The Procurement Problem Nobody Talks About

Your ops manager is reviewing T&M contracts between site visits. Your project manager is running RFQs from their truck. Your office admin is qualifying sub-contractors using a spreadsheet from 2019.

Sound familiar? You're not alone. Most mid-size oil & gas and construction companies in Alberta don't have a dedicated procurement person — and it shows.

**Pain point 1 — Cost leakage is invisible**
When nobody's watching, costs creep. Rate escalations buried in contract amendments. Duplicate vendor agreements. Blanket POs that haven't been reconciled in 18 months. The average company loses 8-12% of their contractor spend to contract leakage they don't even know about.

**Pain point 2 — Compliance gaps are a liability**
COR, ISN, WCB, Avetta, ComplyWorks — contractor compliance isn't optional, and it isn't simple. When compliance tracking falls to whoever has five spare minutes, things slip through. And when they slip, you're the one holding the liability.

**Pain point 3 — Your people are stretched too thin**
Your PM is good at managing projects. Your ops lead is good at running operations. But procurement is its own discipline — with its own skill set, its own tools, and its own pitfalls. Asking your team to do procurement on the side is like asking your PM to do your accounting. They can try, but you wouldn't like the results.

**Pain point 4 — Hiring full-time doesn't make sense (yet)**
A full-time procurement specialist costs $90,000-$120,000 a year plus benefits. For most companies in this space, that's hard to justify — especially when project loads fluctuate. You need the expertise without the overhead.

We manage the procurement work your team shouldn't have to do. From RFPs to rate negotiations to contractor compliance, we handle the details so your team can focus on the work that actually moves your projects forward.

Sourcing, qualifying, and managing the contractors and service providers your projects depend on. We handle the process end-to-end so you get the right vendor at the right price — every time.

Time & materials contracts are where most companies bleed money. We review, negotiate, and administer your T&M agreements so rate escalations, scope creep, and billing errors don't eat your margins.

**Service card 3 — Vendor Evaluation & Qualification**
Not all vendors are created equal. We evaluate capability, compliance, safety records, pricing, and track performance over time — so you stop guessing and start choosing.

**Service card 4 — Spend Analysis & Reporting**
You can't cut costs you can't see. We categorize and analyze your contractor spend, identify where money's going, and show you exactly where the savings are.

From scope development to bid evaluation to award, we manage your competitive bidding process. Clear specs, fair evaluations, better pricing.

Western Canada's energy sector runs on contracted services — and managing those contractors takes dedicated procurement expertise. We work with operators, midstream companies, and oilfield service providers to manage everything from drilling services procurement and facility maintenance contracts to turnaround vendor coordination and equipment rentals.
