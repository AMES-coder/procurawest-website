Tier 01 · Foundation

Foundation

For companies starting to formalize their procurement. Expert guidance, a clear assessment, and the support to start doing procurement right.

Pricing based on company size and scope
Book a call →

What you get

You know you need better procurement processes, but you're not ready for a full-time hire. Foundation gives you expert oversight and mentorship so your team makes better procurement decisions from day one.

  • On-call procurement guidance & mentorship — direct access to our procurement team when your team needs it
  • Vendor evaluation & qualification support — we help you assess capability, compliance, safety records, and pricing
  • Procurement process assessment — a clear picture of where you stand and what needs to change
  • Basic spend categorization — understand where your money is going at a high level
  • Monthly check-in call — regular touchpoint to review priorities and progress
Tier 02 · Operations

Operations

For companies with active projects needing hands-on procurement support — not just advice from the sidelines.

Pricing based on project count and complexity
Talk to us →

Everything in Foundation, plus

You've got projects running and contractors in the field. You need someone managing your RFXs, reviewing your contracts, and tracking vendor performance — not just advising from the sidelines.

  • RFX development & management — from scope development to bid evaluation to award
  • Contract review & negotiation support — every contract reviewed before signature, rates benchmarked
  • T&M contract administration — rate escalations, scope creep, and billing errors caught before they hit your margin
  • Vendor performance tracking — scorecards, KPIs, and quarterly business reviews
  • Invoice review & three-way matching — invoices validated against contracted rates, POs, and field receipts
  • Bi-weekly procurement calls — stay in the loop without doing the legwork
  • Procurement documentation & filing — everything organized, auditable, and accessible

Everything in Operations, plus

We embed into your operations, manage your entire contractor lifecycle, and deliver the reporting your leadership team needs. This is what it looks like to have a procurement department — without building one internally.

  • Complete spend analysis & reporting — categorized, visualized, and delivered to your leadership team monthly
  • Sub-contractor management & onboarding — we handle the full contractor lifecycle from qualification to offboarding
  • Compliance tracking (COR, ISN, WCB) — every platform, every requirement, always current
  • Supply chain risk assessment — single-source dependencies, lead time risks, and contingency planning
  • Monthly executive procurement reports — the numbers your board and leadership team need to see
  • Full invoice validation & vendor dispute resolution — we handle the hard conversations so you don't have to
  • Platform management (Avetta, ISNetworld, ComplyWorks) — fully managed across every platform your clients require
  • On-site support included — boots on the ground when the project needs it
